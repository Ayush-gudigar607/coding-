Python is a high-level, interpreted programming language known for its readability and versatility. It supports multiple programming paradigms, including procedural, object-oriented, and functional programming. Python's extensive standard library and active community contribute to its popularity in various fields such as web development, data analysis, artificial intelligence, scientific computing, and more. With its simple syntax and ease of learning, Python has become a preferred choice for both beginners and experienced developers.
